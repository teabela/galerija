About the Artist

Meet the artist

Mile Belančić, BelaMi

Bajša, Vojvodina, Serbia

Background

I create handmade mounted photographs and photo lamps from my studio in Bajša, Northern Serbia. Each piece combines original photography with metal or leather mounting, welded frames, and intentional oxidation. These aren't just prints — they're objects that carry the weight and texture of industrial craft.

"The material is part of the message. Steel rusts, leather ages, brass develops patina — just like the subjects I photograph."

Process

Photography → Digital editing → Printing on metal or leather → Hand mounting → Welding details → Controlled oxidation → Each piece unique
